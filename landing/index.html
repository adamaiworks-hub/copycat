FAQ

Questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need any coding experience? +
No. CopyCat installs with a single command and runs from a dashboard in your browser. No terminal, no coding, no config files.
What is Polymarket? +
Polymarket is the world's largest prediction market — a platform where people bet real money on the outcome of world events. Top traders regularly make millions per week.
How does copy trading work? +
CopyCat tracks the top 10 traders by weekly profit. Every time one of them places a trade, CopyCat automatically places the same trade in your account — $10 flat per trade by default.
Is my money safe? +
CopyCat has a built-in daily loss limit (default $100/day) that automatically stops trading if things go wrong. You control your own wallet at all times — CopyCat never holds your funds.
What's the difference between Monthly and Pro? +
Monthly copies the top 5 traders with flat $10 trades. Pro copies up to 20 traders, lets you customize trade sizing per trader, and unlocks the analytics dashboard with P&L charts and per-trader breakdowns.
What computer do I need? +
CopyCat runs on Mac and Windows with Python 3.9+. It runs locally on your machine — no cloud subscription required.
